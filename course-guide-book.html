Introduction

Courses at Camp Half-Blood range from Survival and Medical Training to Combat to Arts & Crafts. To enroll, you must meet the requirements. These include your class year, and items you’ll need for the class. Once you enroll, you will be able to complete work for the week we are currently in, and previous. At the end of each “year” (11 weeks) you can complete a short final exam to finish the year. You need to complete 6 exams each year to graduate to the next year!

This guide lists classes available by year, and the items you’ll need for each. You can earn money by getting a site job, earning interest in the bank, or by completing riddles and anagrams at Connor & Travis’ Pranking Schemes and Dionysus’ Daily Jibes.

If you have any problems, you can contact the instructor for the course via Iris Message.
