«–Eoin Parrwyn–»
- Biographical -

Eoin Parrwyn was born to a small family of high Elvish society. She has two younger brothers, but as the oldest child, Eoin was expected to marry well and bring honor as a strong warrior and bearer of magic. Eoin did two thirds: she was a renowned fighter, highly skilled with a longbow and known for her speed and ability to shoot well while moving, and she was excellent at chanted magiks. Marriage, however, did not come as naturally. After a series of failed betrothals, Eoin found herself deeply in love with one lifechanging human. This was, of course, the beginning of Eoin’s fall from grace.

Against her family’s wishes, Eoin pursued a relationship with the human, Thompson. The pair eventually eloped, and lived together for Thompson’s entire lifetime. When she passed, Eoin was left with a small cottage built by hand in the woods, a ridiculously eager yellow dog, and no family to return to.

She joined the army after an encampment of wounded elves traveled near her woods. She hoped to regain respect among her family, but knew she would never willingly return to them. Her skills of survival learned at Thompson’s side would be useful in the coming battles.
