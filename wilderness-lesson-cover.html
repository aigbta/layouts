Wilderness Survival
YEAR THREE: WEAPONS, TOOLS, EQUIPMENT
In your first year, the course required matches and a pocketknife. You've seen how these can be used. This year, we'll throw them out. What happens if you are stranded with nothing but the clothes on your back? How do you cook and hunt? How do you defend yourself?

We'll learn methods for creating tools from only found materials. This course is essential to anyone who may find themselves unprepared.
